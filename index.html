ABOUT

The Pond is an online platform that allows organizations to verify information about customers, members, or applicants through a user-friendly data entry process. Organizations can then manage the information on our platform or through our API.

ADVANTAGES:

Access to more data sources than a traditional verification service (university, employment, financial standing, etc.)

No technical integration required. You simply select the data points you want to capture, and then send a link that will guide individuals through the process.

Simple and intuitive interface for viewing, tracking, and analyzing customers.

HOW IT WORKS

THE PROCESS:

  • Your organization creates an account on The Pond and creates an a new Verification Group consisting of the data points and free response questions you want your customers to answer.
  • A custom link is generated that you can post on your website, blog, or in an email, etc.
  • An individual clicks on the link and is routed to our user-friendly data entry process. Once they've entered all of their data, the individual is rerouted back to a URL of your choice.
  • Once the data has been verified, you can view the individual's information in your Verification Group dashboard.

DATA VERIFICATION:

  • Every individual's information is verified using third-party sources (bank account, college transcript, tax returns, etc.). We do not depend on user-provided responses.
  • We can verity multiple data points at differing levels of precision depending on the need.
  • Our data verification flow includes a live chat to help individuals with any issues they might encounter.
  • After individuals enter their data, our team at The Pond verifies the information within a couple of hours.
